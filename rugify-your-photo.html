Cleaning and Maintenance Tips

To maintain the vibrancy of your personalized rug, it is essential to consider the best cleaning techniques suited to its size and material. Regular vacuuming can help remove dust and debris while preserving the quality of your design. If you chose to rugify your photo in a striking landscape orientation, ensure that you follow specific care instructions to avoid damaging the intricate details. Using gentle cleaners specifically designed for rugs can help maintain the image quality, especially for those featuring movies or personal memories.

Protecting your piece from spills and stains is vital for long-lasting enjoyment in your home. Implementing simple strategies like using a protective spray can make a significant difference. Should accidental spills occur, promptly blotting the area instead of scrubbing can help prevent permanent damage. Take note of the shipping guidelines provided upon purchasing your customized rug to understand specific maintenance recommendations.
