Cleaning Techniques for Handwoven Photo Rugs

Handwoven photo rugs are cherished for their intricate designs and ability to add warmth underfoot. To maintain the beauty of these unique pieces, regular cleaning is essential. Vacuuming gently will help remove dust and dirt from the surface without damaging the fibers. Special attention should be given to the areas with images, as they might require a more careful approach to preserve the details and colors.

For deeper cleaning, consider using a mild soap solution and a soft cloth. Dab the affected areas lightly rather than scrubbing, which can distort the patterns or fray the edges. Spills should be addressed promptly to prevent staining. Following these cleaning techniques will ensure that your handwoven photo rugs continue to enhance the ambiance of your home for years to come.
